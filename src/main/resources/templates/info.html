Stop food waste

Facts

Food waste in the Danish sector constitute 227.000 tons per year. In comparison, the total food waste from households is 261,000 tons per year. The largest food waste in the service sector comes from the retail trade, which spills 163,000 tons per year,
corresponding to 72% of the food sector's food waste. Of the retail trade's food waste, specialshops such as butchers, fish shops and greengrocers account for 12,000 tons per year. while non-specialized stores such as kiosks, grocery stores, supermarkets and department stores account for 151,000 tons per year.
